Background & Approach

A different kind of appraiser

With over 25 years of experience valuing Bay Area residential properties, I bring both an appraiser's analytical approach and a broker's market perspective to every assignment. That combination is rare — and it directly benefits the people I work with.

I understand that many valuation requests come during important financial decisions. Whether you're settling an estate, navigating a property division, or preparing to sell, my role is to provide clarity so you can move forward with confidence.

I know Contra Costa County — the waterfront communities, the Tri-Valley, the Delta properties, the suburban neighborhoods of the East Bay — and I bring that local knowledge to every assignment. My goal is always the same: give you a credible, well-supported opinion of value and explain it in plain language.

Who I work with

My clients come to me at important moments — settling a loved one's estate, navigating a property division, preparing to sell a family home. I take that responsibility seriously and communicate clearly throughout the process.

I work regularly with:

  • CPAs and tax professionals who need date of death valuations and estate documentation
  • Financial advisors and wealth managers supporting clients through estate distributions
  • Estate attorneys handling probate and trust administration
  • Divorce mediators and collaborative divorce professionals
  • REALTORS® representing clients in estate and divorce situations
  • Homeowners and families who simply need to know what their property is worth
